How to read this

  1. Start with Movers this quarter — what moved in ownership.
  2. Click a company (e.g. ICICIBANK) — see its ownership structure, named holders, and who’s accumulating.
  3. Click a promoter group (e.g. ADANI) — see every company it controls, with stakes and the family behind it.
  4. Click an investor (e.g. LIC) — see their full NIFTY-500 book, position by position.
Methodology. Point-in-time SEBI shareholding-pattern data across ~501 NIFTY-500 companies, entity-resolved into promoters, promoter groups, institutional investors, people (4,372 individuals) and listed cross-holdings — every figure linked to its source filing, nothing interpolated. Internal use.
